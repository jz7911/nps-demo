What's the main reason for your score?
Automated branch logic
Score 9–10 — Promoter
Fire promoter_identified event. Referral invite workflow starts. $25 link sent automatically.
Score 7–8 — Passive
Score saved to contact record. No immediate follow-up. Monitored for next NPS cycle.
Score 0–6 — Detractor
Recovery email queued. Contact added to at-risk segment. Staff alert flagged for follow-up call.
NPS resend logic
If a member doesn't respond within 7 days, a follow-up NPS email is sent automatically. Resend cadence: every 60 days for non-responders. Promoters and detractors exit the resend cycle after responding.
